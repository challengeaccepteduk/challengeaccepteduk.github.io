About us

Download today

The Challenge Accepted app helps people discover, track and complete all their personal challenges in one place. Such as:

Health & Fitness challenges e.g. 30 day squat challenge or 30 day cardio challenge
Passion challenges e.g. see every premiership football team
Challenges to do at home e.g. 30 day yoga challenge or crafting ideas
New habits e.g. cut down on drinking or get 30mins of exercise a day
Custom challenges e.g. 12 books I'm going to read this year

Business model

For partners, this means a new route to talk to customers knowing that we’re aligning them with users who are passionate about their product or industry. Brands can partner with Challenge Accepted to create their own company challenge, sponsor challenges or categories, and promote deals to customers that help with their passions e.g. a cinema offer to help users complete their film challenge.

About Challenge Accepted

We are on a mission to give people the inspiration and tools to complete personal challenges, no matter how big or small. With hundreds of challenges for inspiration to start on Challenge Accepted, plus the ability to create your own challenge from scratch.

The Challenge Accepted app was created by Co-Founders Paul Johnson and Steph Mandeville during lockdown and launched in July 2020. Just 2 weeks into the launch, Challenge accepted won Small Business Sunday (SBS) by Retail Entrepreneur Theo Paphitis to help grow Challenge Accepted with access to the SBS network in the UK.

Challenge Accepted are now looking to work with brands to help engage their customers with their brand in a fun and unique way. Please get in touch if you’d like to discuss how we can work together.

The Challenge Accepted app is now available to download for free on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
