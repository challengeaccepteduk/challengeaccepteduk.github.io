Creating fun and interactive challenges

Challenge Accepted creates fun and engaging challenges for people to take part in from health and fitness to travel, theatre and more. We help thousands of people stay motivated with what they start.

Supporting UK charities

Since launch, we've had over 13,000 people take part virtual fundraising challenges on the Challenge Accepted app.

We support UK charities by hosting virtual fundraising events on the app to help participants keep track and stay motivated throughout the campaign.

If you are a registered UK charity get in touch with us to see how we can support your virtual fundraising challenges.
